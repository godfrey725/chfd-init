# Completed Plans

Move finished plans here once their work is done.

## Purpose
Use completed plans as a searchable record of what changed, why it changed, and how it was verified.

## Expectations
- Preserve the final plan text that matched the delivered work.
- Add brief completion notes if they help future readers.
- Prefer leaving a historical trail over deleting useful planning context.
- If the implementation changed materially from the original plan, update the plan before filing it here.

## What Good Records Capture
- the final execution path
- major deviations and why they were made
- verification that was actually run
- any follow-up that moved into tech debt or later plans
